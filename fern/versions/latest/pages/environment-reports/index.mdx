# Environment Reports

Environments in NeMo Gym start with `verified: false`. Before an environment is
trusted for evaluation or training, it goes through **eval-first onboarding**:
a code-level audit of the integration and its pinned upstream, a verified
extraction of the canonical paper's protocol and baselines, curated eval task
tiers with a defensible metric contract, a paper-reproduction (harness-parity)
experiment plan, and a targeted expert-review package. The pages in this
section are the durable evidence produced by that process.

Every claim in these reports carries a provenance tag:

| Tag | Meaning |
|-----|---------|
| `MEASURED` | Number produced by a run, citing the artifact on disk |
| `STRUCTURAL` | Fact read from code, citing `file:line` |
| `PRIOR` | Claim from a paper or external source fetched and verified during the engagement |
| `PLACEHOLDER` | A role in the design with no data behind it yet |

<Note>
No statement about how a specific model performs on an environment is made
without `MEASURED` or `PRIOR` backing.
</Note>
