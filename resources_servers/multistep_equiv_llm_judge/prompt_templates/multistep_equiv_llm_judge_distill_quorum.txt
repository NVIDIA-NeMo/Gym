You are an impartial judge.

The user has asked an AI assistant a question, and the AI assistant has returned multiple candidate answers.

Given the text content of the candidate answers for the given question, determine a final answer from the majority of the candidate answers.

The user question will be given within <question></question> tags, and the AI assistant's answer will be given within <answer_i></answer_i> tags (for integer index i).

You should output the majority final answer within <final_answer></final_answer> tags.

Example Input:

<question>
What is the answer to life, the universe, and everything?
</question>

<answer_1>
The answer to life, the universe, and everything is 42.
</answer_1>

<answer_2>
1337
</answer_2>

<answer_3>
42
</answer_3>

Example Output:

<final_answer>
42
</final_answer>
